Products

High Performance Architechtural Grade Aluminum-Framed Windows & Doors

When selecting windows and doors, a long-term partnership between the manufacturer and installer is essential. We only offer products made locally in the United States, made only by brands we know and trust.

For over a half-century, Softer Lite’s focus has been High Performance Architectural Grade Aluminum-Framed Windows and Doors. The windows we offer vary from single hung, double hung, horizontal sliding, casement, projected, and fixed. Doors are either sliding glass doors or terrace swing doors. We understand Performance, Usability, Code Requirements, Aesthetics, Finishes, Handle Types, and Your Budget are all important factors when choosing the right products.

We are here to make the process as easy as possible for you. Whether you know exactly what you need, or you’re just starting to explore your building’s options, you’ve come to the right place. We’ll pinpoint exactly which model numbers and specifications are best for your project. From there, the windows and doors are custom-made to fit your building’s unique needs. Softer Lite will take care of the rest because we view peace of mind as an installation standard.

Softer Lite Window Company prides itself in accommodating each project's specific needs, including but not limited to: ADA Compliance (Americans with Disabilities Act), LEED Compliance, Fire-Rating, STC Rating (Sound Transmission Class).
