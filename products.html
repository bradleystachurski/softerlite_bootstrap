High Performance Architechtural Grade Aluminum-Framed Windows & Doors

When selecting windows and doors, a long-term partnership between the manufacturer and installer is essential. We only offer products made locally in the United States, made only by brands we know and trust.

Windows

Softer Lite offers single hung, double hung, horizontal sliding, casement, projected, and fixed windows. Whether you know exactly the model numbers and color you need, or you’re just starting to explore your building’s options, you’ve come to the right place. At Softer Lite, we understand Performance, Usability, Energy Efficiency, Code Requirements, Aesthetics, and Your Budget are all important factors when choosing window types.

We are here to work with you to pinpoint exactly which model and specifications are best for your project. From there, the windows are custom-made to fit your building’s unique needs. Softer Lite will take care of the rest because we view peace of mind as an installation standard.

Doors

Softer Lite offers both Sliding Glass Doors and Terrace Swing Doors. As with choosing windows, we know Performance, Usability, Energy Efficiency, Code Requirements, Aesthetics, and Your Budget are all important factors when choosing the doors for your entire building.

Whether you know exactly what you’re looking for, or want to explore different Performances, Finishes, and Handle Types, you’ve come to the right place.
